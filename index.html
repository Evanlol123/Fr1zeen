Fr1zeen

Planned Release Date: November 16

What is it?

FR1ZEEN is an expliot that can unenroll an enterprise enrolled chromebook that has a leaked RMA shim on any ChromeOS version. It boots from a USB flash drive from a edited RMA shim to a GUI that gives you the option to then unenroll your Chromebook

How Fr1zeen was found

The FR1ZEEN exploit was discovered by a group of ethical hackers during a routine security audit of school Chromebooks. They identified a vulnerability in the update engine that could be exploited to remove enterprise enrollment, leading to the creation of the exploit

Prerequisites:

  1. A brain
  2. An enterprise enrolled chromebook with a working leaked RMA shim
  3. Access to an image flasher like Chromebook Recovery Utility
  4. A USB flash drive with at least 8gb of storage

Notes:

We are still experimenting with the exploit and testing it for bugs. Yes, this does work on devices witha TI50 chip.
