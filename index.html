Fr1zeen

Planned Release Date: November 16

What is it?

FR1ZEEN is an expliot that can unenroll a school chromebook on any ChromeOS version that has a leaked RMA shim. It boots from a USB flash drive from a edited RMA shim to a GUI that gives you the option to then unenroll your Chromebook

How Fr1zeen was found

The FR1ZEEN exploit was discovered by a group of ethical hackers during a routine security audit of school Chromebooks. They identified a vulnerability in the update engine that could be exploited to remove enterprise enrollment, leading to the creation of the exploit

Prerequisites:

  1. A brain
  2. An enterprise enrolled chromebook with a leaked rma shim
  3. A personal computer that can run Google Chrome
  4. A USB flash drive with at least 8gb of storage
